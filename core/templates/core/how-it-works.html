As of now, our capacity allows to produce only 30 meals per day. We may decline to accept order if we receive order exceeding our capacity.
Payment can be done online at the time of placing order by credit card (VISA, Master) or mobile money (MTN, Airtel) only. We do not collect cash on delivery. Postpaid system is available for the customers who wish to pay weekly or monthly. (link)
First Time Customer We will make a call or send e-mail to confirm delivery details every time when we receive order from new customers in order to deliver bento without mistake. Attempt Delivery Notice We will issue this notice if we attempted our delivery in your absence. Please call us as soon as you return to your home/office to arrange re-delivery. Discount Coupon We will issue this coupon if we noticed that we damaged bento during delivery. We will send coupon number by e-mail later. Please feel free to contact us if you do not find the coupon while you received damaged bento, but preferably with photograph. Disclaimer We deliver our bento using motorcycles. We may fail to deliver or deliver late due to the following reasons. - In the case where driving motorcycles is difficult due to heavy rainfall, thunderstorm, strike, riot, roadblock. - In the case we received wrong contact, delivery address. - In the case receiver is not available at the time of delivery.
This is a payment system for customers who wish to pay for our service weekly or monthly depending on your preference 1. Customers deposit certain amount to us. 2. We shall make ID for postpaid customers. 3. Customer shall select "postpaid customer" on the payment page, and enter your ID and password. 4. Customers are free to order bento not exceeding the amount of deposit. 5. We shall provide you weekly or monthly invoice according to your preference 6. Customers can make payment from the following choice. Online settlement * Mobile money / MTN or Airtel only * Credit card / VISA or MASTER only Offline settlement * Cash payment at our office * Bank transfer or deposit to our bank account
