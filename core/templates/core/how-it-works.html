*Our Service
We provide "Lunchbox delivery service" of Japanese bento. * Day and time
We deliver only once daily; at lunch time from Monday to Friday excluding public holidays. * Timing
Orders are expected before 7pm the previous day, then bento is delivered to homes/offices between 1200 - 1300 hours. Order can be placed up to two weeks in advance. * Quantity
Currently, our capacity allows production of 30 meals per day. We may decline orders that exceed our capacity. * Cancellation
Our cancellation charges are as follows;
1. After 7pm of the previous date: 100%
2. After 7pm of two days before 7pm of the previous day: 50%
3. Before 7pm of two days befor: 0%
* Payment can be done online at the time of placing order by credit card (VISA, Master) or mobile money (MTN, Airtel) only.
* We do not collect cash on delivery.
* Postpaid system is available for the customers who wish to pay weekly or monthly.

 *Self Collection
You are free to collect bento from our kitchen at any time between 1200 and 1300 hours. Delivery fee will not be charged in this case.
 *Attempt Delivery Notice
We will issue this notice if we attempted our delivery in your absence. Please call us as soon as you return to your home/office to arrange re-delivery. *Refund Coupon
This coupon will be issued once we notice that the bento has been damaged during delivery. The coupon number will be sent later via e-mail. Please feel free to contact us if you do not find the coupon but receive damaged bento, preferably with a photograph. But we do not replace bento due to our manufacturing capacity. *Disclaimer
Bento is delivered using motorcycles. We may fail to deliver or deliver late due to the following reasons:
- In the case where driving motorcycles is difficult due to heavy rainfall, thunderstorm, strike, riot, roadblock.
- receiving the wrong contact or delivery information.
- If receiver is not available at the time of delivery.
This payment system is for customers who wish to pay for our service weekly or monthly depending on their preference.
1. Customers give us a deposit.
2. An I.D. is made for postpaid customers.
3. Customers shall select "postpaid customer" on the payment page and enter their I.D. and password.
4. Customers are free to order bento not exceeding the amount of deposit.
5. Monthly/weekly invoices will be provided to customers according to their preference.
