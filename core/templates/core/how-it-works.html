*Our Service We provide "Lunchbox delivery service" of Japanese bento. * Day and time We make a delivery only once a day at lunch time from Monday to Friday exclude public holidays . * Timing We expect orders from customers before 7pm of the previous day.
Then we will deliver bento to your home/office between 12-13 O'clock. Order can be placed up to two weeks in advance. Then we will deliver bento to your home/office between 12-13 O'clock. * Quantity As of now, our capacity allows to produce only 30 meals per day. We may decline to accept order if we receive order exceeding our capacity. * Cancellation Our cancellation charges are as follows; 1. After 7pm of the previous date: 100% 2. After 7pm of two days before 7pm of the previous day: 50% 3. Before 7pm of two days befor: 0%
* Payment can be done online at the time of placing order by credit card (VISA, Master) or mobile money (MTN, Airtel) only. * We do not collect cash on delivery. * Postpaid system is available for the customers who wish to pay weekly or monthly.
*First Time Customer We will make a call or send e-mail to confirm delivery details every time when we receive order from new customers in order to deliver bento without mistake. * Area (Map will be provided later) *Attempt Delivery Notice We will issue this notice if we attempted our delivery in your absence. Please call us as soon as you return to your home/office to arrange re-delivery. *Discount Coupon We will issue this coupon if we noticed that we damaged bento during delivery. We will send coupon number by e-mail later. Please feel free to contact us if you do not find the coupon while you received damaged bento, but preferably with photograph. But we do not replace bento due to our manufacturing capacity. *Disclaimer We deliver our bento using motorcycles. We may fail to deliver or deliver late due to the following reasons. - In the case where driving motorcycles is difficult due to heavy rainfall, thunderstorm, strike, riot, roadblock. - In the case we received wrong contact, delivery address. - In the case receiver is not available at the time of delivery.

Online settlement * Mobile money / MTN or Airtel only * Credit card / VISA or MASTER only Offline settlement * Cash payment at our office * Bank transfer or deposit to our bank account Deposit shall be refunded as soon as you send us the cancellation of this postpaid system.
