*Our Service We provide "Lunchbox delivery service" of Japanese bento. * Day and time We deliver only once daily; at lunch time from Monday to Friday excluding public holidays. * Timing Orders are expected before 7pm the previous day, then bento is delivered to homes/offices between 1200 - 1300 hours. Order can be placed up to two weeks in advance. * Quantity Currently, our capacity allows production of 30 meals per day. We may decline orders that exceed our capacity. * Cancellation Our cancellation charges are as follows; 1. After 7pm of the previous date: 100% 2. After 7pm of two days before 7pm of the previous day: 50% 3. Before 7pm of two days befor: 0%
* Payment can be done online at the time of placing order by credit card (VISA, Master) or mobile money (MTN, Airtel) only. * We do not collect cash on delivery. * Postpaid system is available for the customers who wish to pay weekly or monthly.
